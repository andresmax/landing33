FAQ

Everything you need to know about getting started, staying consistent, and making gratitude part of your daily life.

Is {{site.title}} really free?
Yes! {{site.title}} is completely free to download and use. All core features including daily journaling, reminders, and prompts are available at no cost. No hidden fees, no subscriptions required.
How do I start a gratitude practice?
Just download {{site.title}} and write your first entry. Start with something simple - one thing you're grateful for today. Set a daily reminder and let the app guide you with thoughtful prompts. Need inspiration? Check out our 100+ gratitude journal prompts. Consistency matters more than perfection.
Is my journal private?
Absolutely. Your entries are stored locally on your device and protected by Face ID or Touch ID. We never store your data on our servers, and there's zero tracking or analytics. Your thoughts stay completely private. Learn more about our privacy-first approach.
Can I sync across my Apple devices?
Yes! {{site.title}} uses iCloud to securely sync your gratitude entries across all your Apple devices. Write on your iPhone, read on your iPad - your journal goes wherever you go.
What if I miss a day?
That's okay! {{site.title}} is designed to encourage, not pressure. Missing a day doesn't break your practice. Just pick up where you left off. Progress isn't about perfection - it's about showing up when you can. Learn about the science-backed benefits of gratitude journaling.
What devices is {{site.title}} available on?
{{site.title}} is available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Your entries sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices via iCloud, so you can journal wherever inspiration strikes.
Is there an Android version?
{{site.title}} is currently available exclusively on iOS. We're focused on creating the best possible experience for iPhone and iPad users. Sign up for updates to be notified if that changes.
