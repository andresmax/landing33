The Short Version

We don't store your data. {{site.title}} has no servers or databases. Your journal entries, photos, and personal information are stored only on your device and, if you choose to enable iCloud sync, in your own personal iCloud account managed by Apple. We cannot access, read, or retrieve your journal entries.

Information You Provide

  • Name: Optionally provided to personalize greetings. Stored only on your device and your iCloud account (if sync is enabled).
  • Email Address: Optionally provided to receive occasional updates. This is the only data sent to an external service (ConvertKit).
  • Journal Entries: The gratitude entries you write. Stored only on your device and your iCloud account (if sync is enabled).
  • Photos: Images you attach to entries (Pro feature). Stored only on your device and your iCloud account (if sync is enabled).

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Contacts or calendar data

Data Storage

Your Rights
