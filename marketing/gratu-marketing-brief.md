Marketing Brief for Gratu - Gratitude Journal App

  About Gratu:
  Gratu is a minimalist gratitude journaling app for iOS that helps users build a daily gratitude practice. The app focuses on simplicity, privacy, and beautiful design.

  Core Value Proposition:
  - Reduces stress and anxiety through daily gratitude reflection
  - Improves sleep quality and overall happiness
  - Builds emotional resilience through habit formation
  - Privacy-first: all data stays on-device or in user's personal iCloud

  Pricing Model:
  - Free tier: 30 journal entries, daily prompts, reminders, biometric lock, dark mode
  - Pro: $2.99/month or $19.99/year - unlocks unlimited entries, iCloud sync, photo attachments, 7-day and 30-day challenges, and social sharing

  Current Situation:
  - Live on the App Store with minimal visibility
  - Website: gratu.app
  - Goal: Increase daily downloads and convert free users to Pro subscribers

  Target Audience:
  - People interested in mindfulness, mental health, wellness, and personal development
  - Ages 18-45, primarily women
  - Users of meditation apps, journaling apps, and self-care content consumers
  - Instagram/TikTok users following wellness and productivity creators

  Marketing Channels:
  1. Instagram/TikTok (Primary): Short-form video content, UGC, Reels, influencer partnerships
  2. ASO: App Store optimization for keywords like gratitude, journal, mindfulness, wellness, mental health, self care
  3. SEO: Organic content for gratu.app targeting gratitude practice, journaling benefits, etc.

  3. ASO keyword strategy and App Store listing optimization
  4. SEO content calendar and blog post ideas for gratu.app
  5. Conversion optimization ideas to move free users to Pro
  6. Creative ad concepts for paid social if we scale
