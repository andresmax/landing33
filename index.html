FAQ

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common things people ask us.

How does the free trial work?
You get 14 days of full access to all features, no credit card required. At the end of your trial, you can choose a plan that fits your needs or continue with our free tier.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any difference in billing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, and are SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data is backed up daily across multiple regions.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team for a full refund.
How do I get support?
We offer email support for all plans, with priority chat support for Pro and Enterprise customers. Our average response time is under 2 hours during business hours.
